## Keys:

### UI:

    F:          Toggle FullScreen

### Moving:

    G:          5 steps back
    LEFT/A/H:   step back
    RIGHT/D/J:  step forward
    K:          5 steps forwards
    1-9:        moving in direction
    -/+:        changing direction

    m/b/c:      take card from bank
    space/x:    pieck to put
    e/z:        end turn
